MAS Monetary Policy Statement - April 2026

Published Date: 14 April 2026

Summary of decision

  • MAS will therefore increase slightly the rate of appreciation of the S$NEER policy band.
  • There will be no change to its width and the level at which it is centred.
  • GDP growth in 2026 as a whole is likely to step down from the above-trend pace of growth recorded in 2025.
  • The forecasts for MAS Core Inflation and CPI-All Items inflation have both been raised to 1.5–2.5%, from 1.0–2.0% previously.
  • MAS stands ready to curb excessive volatility in the S$NEER.
